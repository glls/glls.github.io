George Litos

Senior Software Engineer

With over two decades of experience as a software developer, Georgios Litos is a seasoned professional based in Thessaloniki, Greece. He has a proven track record in leading and participating in research projects and developing innovative solutions.

Georgios’s career highlights include:

  • Research Assistant at the Information Technologies Institute (ITI) of Centre for Research and Technology - Hellas (CERTH), contributing to numerous EU and GSRI-funded projects.
  • Senior Backend Developer at Schoox, an enterprise LMS and talent development platform.
  • Software Architect at Hypertech SA, providing full-stack development and server administration services.
  • Team Lead at AgroApps PC, heading the IT R&D department for agricultural solutions.
  • Senior Software Engineer at Trinity Systems, specializing in hardware RFID technologies and software development.
