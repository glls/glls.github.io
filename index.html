Biography

Georgios Litos was born in Naoussa, Greece. He has more than twenty years of professional experience as a software developer. He currently lives in Thessaloniki.

George worked as a Research Assistant in the Information Technologies Institute (ITI) of Centre for Research and Technology - Hellas (CERTH) from Jan 2000 to Aug 2017. He has participated in several research projects funded by the European Union and the General Secretariat for Research and Innovation (GSRI).

He worked as a Senior Backend Developer at Schoox, an enterprise LMS and talent development platform from September 2017 to November 2020.

On December 2020 George joined HYPERTECH SA as Software Architect providing full-stack development services and server administration.

George led a team of enthusiasts at the IT R&D department of AgroApps PC, a company that provided advanced solutions for the agricultural sector from April 2022 to August 2023.

George Litos has a proven track record of success in leading and participating in research projects, with a focus on developing innovative solutions.
