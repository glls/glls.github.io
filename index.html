Biography

Georgios Litos was born in Naoussa, Greece, in 1973, he has more than twenty years of professional experience in software development.

George worked as a Research Assistant in the Informatics and Telematics Institute (ITI) of Centre for Research and Technology - Hellas (CERTH) from Jan 2000 to Aug 2017. He has participated in several research projects funded by the European Union and the Greek Secretariat of Research and Technology.

He worked as a Senior Backend Developer at Schoox, an enterprise LMS and talent development platform from Sep 2017 to Nov 2020.

George is a Software Architect at HYPERTECH SA from Dec 2020.
