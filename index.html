Biography

Georgios Litos was born in Naoussa, Greece, he has more than twenty years of professional experience in software development. He currently lives in Thessaloniki.

George worked as a Research Assistant in the Information Technologies Institute (ITI) of Centre for Research and Technology - Hellas (CERTH) from Jan 2000 to Aug 2017. He has participated in several research projects funded by the European Union and the General Secretariat for Research and Innovation (GSRI).

He worked as a Senior Backend Developer at Schoox, an enterprise LMS and talent development platform from Sep 2017 to Nov 2020.

On December of 2020 George joined HYPERTECH SA as Software Architect providing full-stack development services as well as server administration.

George leads a team of enthusiasts at the IT RnD department of AgroApps PC, a company that provides advanced solutions for the agricultural sector from April 2022.
